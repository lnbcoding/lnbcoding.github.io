Difference between Margin, Border, Padding | Sep 28, 2014

I think of margin as the outside of the element. Border is the actual outline of the element. Padding is the inside of the element and where the content is. Here is a screenshot I did to show the differences between them. When you see the code and the image it should help you visualize it.

I like playing poker so maybe this concept will also help you. Think of a playing card lying on the bottom right, corner edge of a table. The border is the physical edge of the card. The padding is the content's position inside the card, say for example the 7 of hearts and how it is position inside the playing card.

Because the card is lying on the bottom right, edge of the table, the margin left and margin bottom is 0. As you move the card to the right, the margin left will increase. Same thing if you move the card up, the margin bottom will also increase.
